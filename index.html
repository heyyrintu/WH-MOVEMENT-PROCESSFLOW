Plan Basis

Industry-standard relocation logic

The move is structured as a wave-based inter-facility transfer into one destination warehouse. Phase 1 prioritizes Edel movement, Phase 2 clears non-movers, and Phase 3 handles the rest of inventory under manifest, scan, seal, and daily reconciliation control.

Phase 1

Edel movement first, with route-wise staging and strict load control at source and destination.

Phase 2

Non-mover movement next, freeing static stock from source reserve and bulk storage areas.

Phase 3

Move the remaining inventory with final balancing, cutover, residual sweep, and reconciliation.

Control Rule

No truck departs without scan-complete manifest, seal number, dock release, and destination appointment.
